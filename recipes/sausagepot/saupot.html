Sausage Pot

Ingredients

  • 500g sausage, sliced
  • 2 potatoes, diced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add sausage and cook until browned.
  3. Add onion, potatoes, and carrots. Cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Pour in chicken broth, bring to a boil.
  5. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 20 minutes.
  6. Season with salt and pepper. Serve hot.
